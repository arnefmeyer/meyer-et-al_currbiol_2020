The file example_freely_moving.npy contains all data as a single array with columns:
1: timestamps (s)
2: head pitch (deg)
3: head roll (deg)
4: angular head velocity around the pitch axis (deg/s)
5: angular head velocity around the roll axis (deg/s)
6: angular head velocity around the yaw axis (deg/s)
7: pupil pos. left eye horizontal (deg)
8: pupil pos. left eye vertical (deg)
9: pupil pos. right eye horizontal (deg)
10: pupil pos. right eye vertical (deg)
